Phase 1

Marvel's initial phase, introducing the individual characters in their own respective movies before the great culmination of their combined The Avengers in 2012. This set the foundation for all the following phases, including different shows, movies and storylines.

Phase 2

Marvel's second phase added both characters and storylines, including Agents of S.H.I.E.L.D, revealing that Agent Phil Coulson was still alive after The Avengers. The final movie of this phase is Age of Ultron in 2015.
